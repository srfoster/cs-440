# CS440: Artificial Intelligence

Welcome to CS440! This course explores the foundational concepts and techniques of artificial intelligence, from intelligent agents and search algorithms to machine learning and beyond.

## Course Overview

This course provides a comprehensive introduction to artificial intelligence, covering fundamental concepts, algorithms, and paradigms that enable machines to exhibit intelligent behavior. Through hands-on problems and theoretical exploration, students will master core AI techniques including search algorithms, knowledge representation, planning, reasoning under uncertainty, and machine learning fundamentals.

## Topics

### [Module 1: Intelligent Agents and Search](content/chapter-01/)

**Problem Formulation:**
* State spaces and problem representation
* 8-puzzle formulation

**Uninformed Search:**
* Breadth-First Search (BFS)
* Iterative Deepening DFS (IDDFS)
* Maze navigation algorithms

**Informed Search:**
* A* search with admissible heuristics
* Heuristic design and evaluation

---

### [Module 2: Local Search and Optimization](content/chapter-02/)

**Hill Climbing:**
* 8-Queens problem
* Local optima and plateaus

**Stochastic Methods:**
* Simulated annealing
* Genetic algorithms
* Continuous optimization

---

### [Module 3: Adversarial Search and Games](content/chapter-03/)

**Game Playing:**
* Minimax algorithm
* Alpha-beta pruning
* Game tree evaluation

**Stochastic Games:**
* Monte Carlo tree search (MCTS)
* Random rollouts
* Nash equilibrium basics

---

### [Module 4: Constraint Satisfaction Problems](content/chapter-04/)

**CSP Formulation:**
* Variables, domains, and constraints
* Map coloring and Sudoku

**Search and Inference:**
* Backtracking with forward checking
* Arc consistency (AC-3)
* Tree-structured CSPs

---

### [Module 5: Logic and Inference](content/chapter-05/)

**First-Order Logic:**
* Unification algorithm
* Term matching and substitution

**Inference Methods:**
* Forward chaining
* Backward chaining
* Resolution

---

### [Module 6: Probabilistic Reasoning and Learning](content/chapter-06/)

**Bayesian Networks:**
* Exact inference with enumeration
* Likelihood weighting

**Temporal Models:**
* Hidden Markov Models (HMMs)
* Forward algorithm (filtering)
* Particle filtering

**Supervised Learning:**
* Training and evaluation
* Loss functions and optimization

---

### [Module 7: Neural Networks and Transformers](content/chapter-07/)

**Deep Learning Fundamentals:**
* Feedforward networks
* Backpropagation
* Convolutional networks

**Modern Architectures:**
* Self-attention mechanism
* Transformer blocks
* Language model training

---
